A City

This song was originally recorded by Deserts Chang (Anpu). This song encompasses so much content that I can't simply describe the meaning it brings to me. We each walk in the city, interact, and together form the city. The city becomes diverse because of its people. People talk about religion, ideals, entertainment, and many other things. Most importantly, we need to remember that we love each other.
