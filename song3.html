Left Right Hand

This song was originally recorded and released by Leslie Cheung, who was a Hong Kong singer.


The first time I heard this song was covered by Kit Chan, who is a well-known Singapore singer. I was about fourteen years old at that time. I sat in front of the TV and was fascinated by her performance. Despite all these years, I still think it's the most beautiful voice I've ever heard.
